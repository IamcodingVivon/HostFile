The <marquee> tag is used to make text scroll up or down, left or right.
It is written as follows:
<marquee direction="left">The text written here will scroll left to right</marquee>
<marquee direction="right">The text written here will scroll right to left</marquee>
<marquee direction="down">The text written here will scroll down to up</marquee>
<marquee direction="up">The text written here will scroll up to down</marquee>
